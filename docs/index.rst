Welcome to pyFDM's documentation!
=================================

The PyFDM (Python Fuzzy Decision Making) package serves as a versatile toolkit designed to facilitate research and practical implementation in the field of fuzzy decision-making. With a focus on providing a comprehensive suite of tools, PyFDM aims to empower users to model, analyze, and optimize decision processes under uncertainty, offering a centralized resource for fuzzy logic applications and decision support systems.
